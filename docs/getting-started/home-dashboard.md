### 3. Email Deliverability

Monitor the health of your email campaigns to ensure they reach the inbox.

- **Email Sent**: Total number of emails sent.
- **Deliveries**: Number of emails successfully delivered to recipients.
- **Unique Opens**: The number of individual recipients who opened the email at least once.

- **Opens**: The total number of times an email was opened.

<details>

Includes multiple opens by the same recipient

Counts repeated opens on different devices (mobile + desktop)

Shows how often your email was viewed

Evening (desktop)

</details>

- **Unique Clicks**: The number of individual recipients who clicked at least one link in the email.
