# üè† Home Dashboard

The **Home Dashboard** is your command center in C360. It gives you a real-time overview of your business performance, alerts, and critical metrics the moment you log in.

## üìä Dashboard Widgets

### 1. Campaign Pulse Summary

Track the performance of your recent marketing efforts.

- **Campaigns Executed**: Total number of active campaigns.
- **Emails Sent**: Volume of emails delivered.
- **Assets Clicked**: Engagement metrics for your content.
- **Leads Profiled**: Number of new leads generated.
- **Opportunities Generated**: Potential deals created from campaigns.

### 2. Open & Click Rate

Visual charts showing email engagement trends over time.

- **Open Rate**: Percentage of recipients who opened your emails.
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Percentage of recipients who clicked on links.

### 3. Email Deliverability

Monitor the health of your email campaigns to ensure they reach the inbox.

### 4. Opportunity Analytics

Gain insights into your sales pipeline efficiency.

- **Opportunity Source**: Breakdown of where your deals are originating.
- **Sales Performance**: Individual or team performance metrics.
- **Opportunity Revenue**:
  - **Top 10 Open Opportunities**: View the highest-value deals currently in the pipeline.

### 5. Platform Adoption

Track how well your team is utilizing the C360 platform.

- **Leads Added**: New leads entered into the system.
- **Campaigns Added**: Marketing campaigns created.
- **Assets & Emails**: Content generation metrics.
- **Notes & Tasks**: Engagement and activity tracking.

### 6. Upcoming Tasks

A list of your pending to-dos and follow-ups.

- **Priority Indicators**: High, Medium, Low.
- **Due Dates**: Stay on top of deadlines.
