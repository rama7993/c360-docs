# 📡 Social Monitoring

The **Social Monitoring** section in C360 helps you manage your connected social channels, monitor posts, and analyze performance through reports — all in one place.

---

## 📍 Navigation

To access **Social Monitoring** in C360:

1. Go to the **Marketing** menu on the left.
2. Click **Social**.
3. Select the **Social Monitoring** tab.

---

## 📑 Tabs in Social Monitoring

Social Monitoring is divided into three key areas:

- **Channels** – Manage and connect social profiles.
- **Posts** – View, create, and manage social media posts.
- **Reports** – Analyze engagement and performance.

---

## 🌐 Channels

The **Channels** tab lets you connect, view, and manage your social accounts.

- **Supported Platforms**: Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, YouTube.
- **Channel Listing**: Displays all connected accounts with details:
  - Channel name
  - Status (connected/disconnected)
  - Last synced time
- **Actions**:
  - **+ Add Channel** – Connect a new account.
  - **Remove/Disconnect** – Remove an existing account.
  - **Refresh** – Sync data manually.

## 📝 Posts

The **Posts** tab consolidates all posts from your connected channels.

You can create, schedule, and manage posts with multiple status-based views.

## 📊 Reports

The **Reports** tab provides insights into post and channel performance.

- **Engagement Reports** – Likes, comments, shares, clicks.
- **Channel Performance** – Compare engagement across channels.
- **Post Type Analysis** – Image vs video vs link performance.
- **Growth Metrics** – Follower growth and audience reach.

<figure>  
  <img src="media/reports-overview.png" alt="Social monitoring reports dashboard" width="800" />  
  <figcaption>Reports dashboard with engagement and growth insights</figcaption>  
</figure>

:::note  
Reports can be exported in **PDF** or **Excel** formats for sharing with stakeholders.  
:::

---

## ⚙️ Additional Features

- **Search & Filter** – Find posts by keyword, date range, or channel.
- **Bulk Actions** – Delete, reschedule, or publish multiple posts at once.
- **Collaboration** – Assign posts to teammates for follow-up.
- **Notifications** – Get alerts for failed posts or expiring channel connections.

---

## 🎯 Why Use Social Monitoring?

- Centralized control of **all social media activity**.
- Streamlined **content publishing & scheduling**.
- Data-driven insights with **performance reports**.
- Improved **team collaboration** and faster responses.
